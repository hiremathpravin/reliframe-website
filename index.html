Why ReliFrame

Cross-tool traceability

Link FTA basic events to GSN solution nodes. Connect FMEA failure modes to RBD component blocks. Every analysis artefact is traceable across your entire safety case — no manual cross-referencing.

Built-in standards validation

GSN001 through GSN018 checked live as you construct your argument. Violations surface inline — before export, before review. Know your safety case is structurally sound at every save.

Real-time collaboration

Invite team members with role-based access — reviewer, editor, owner. Shared workspaces for cross-functional teams spanning systems, software, and functional safety disciplines.
